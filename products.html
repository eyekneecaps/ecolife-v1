EcoLife Plastic

Professional plastic collection & recycling service.

EcoLife Plastic is a comprehensive recycling service designed to make your life easier and more environmentally friendly. Our trained professionals collect your recyclable plastics directly from your home or business on a schedule that works for you. We sort, process, and ensure that your plastics are responsibly recycled into new products.

By using this service, you contribute directly to reducing landfill waste, minimizing environmental pollution, and promoting sustainable recycling practices. Every pickup helps conserve natural resources and reduces carbon emissions caused by improper disposal of plastics.

Pricing: 450 AED for the first month, 150 AED/month thereafter until you cancel. Easy, hassle-free, and impactful.

EcoLife Can

Professional aluminum & metal can recycling service.

EcoLife Can collects aluminum cans and other recyclable metals, providing an easy and efficient way to manage your recyclables. Our team picks up your items, sorts them, and ensures they are processed correctly to maximize recycling efficiency.

This service helps reduce resource waste, saves energy, and prevents metals from entering landfills. It's perfect for environmentally conscious households and businesses that want to make a real impact while keeping spaces clutter-free.

Pricing: 450 AED for the first month, 150 AED/month thereafter. Join thousands of residents in making recycling simple and meaningful.

EcoLife Card

Scan and log your recycling activity instantly.

The EcoLife Card links directly to your EcoLife account and allows you to instantly log your recycling activity with a simple scan. Every item you recycle is tracked, helping you monitor your environmental impact in real time.

This card motivates and rewards eco-friendly behavior by keeping a clear record of your contributions. Perfect for individuals, families, and schools who want to engage in responsible recycling and sustainability efforts.

Price: 25 AED per card. Simple, practical, and a great way to make your recycling efforts count.
